h1. World

In the world of Alteia, many knew the tale of a promised neverland. One that held untouched riches; where magick and the supernatural is commonplace.

It remained a fairy-tale for many years before a pirate crew discovered the location. That crew brought many of the riches and sold them at a high price.

These otherworldly items and minerals caused many kingdoms to war over the very few amount of resources available. It wasn't long before explorers were able to find this new world.

After that, many people escaped their wartorned homeland and traveled to this new world to start new.

Your story takes place 100 years after settlers and refugees made this new world their home.
